How to Play Factor Facts

1. Multiply!

Two numbers are multiplied together to make a bigger number. For example, 3 × 4 = 12.

2. Find the Missing Number

One of the three circles will be empty. Use the number pad to type the missing number, then press the green checkmark to submit.

3. Scoring

Each correct answer earns points. Later rounds use bigger numbers and are worth more points!

4. One Mistake and You're Out!

A wrong answer ends the game. Try to beat your high score!

5. Tips

If the big number and one factor are shown, try dividing to find the missing factor. You can also use the keyboard to type numbers and press Enter to submit.
